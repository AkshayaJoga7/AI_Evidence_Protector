About AI Evidence Protector

Next-Generation Cryptographic & AI Digital Evidence Custody

🛡️ The Mission

AI Evidence Protector is engineered to protect citizens, whistleblowers, security personnel, and legal professionals by guaranteeing that recorded media remains tamper-evident, verifiable, and legally binding.

🔐 Cryptographic Chain of Custody

When an image or audio clip is captured or uploaded, the system immediately computes a SHA-256 hash digest. Any attempt to modify even a single pixel or audio frame completely alters the hash, immediately flagging tamper alerts.

🤖 Neural AI Verification

Integrated artificial intelligence models analyze file structures, metadata headers, frequency domains, and compression artifacts to detect synthetic manipulation, deepfakes, or unauthorized splicing.
